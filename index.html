Basic branding ideas

CatWorld is a company that sells cat products such as food, supplies, medicines and toys. The potential customers are the people who have cats in their house. Compared to other pet supplies stores that sell many different pets' products, CatWorld focuses on people who only want to find products related to cats. Additionally, the website has an information page that has many cat related articles which can help owners to learn more about their cats.
